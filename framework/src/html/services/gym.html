Your Health & Fitness

Let us help you get strong, balanced and flexible – mentally and physically.

In order to aid you in achieving your fitness goals, we offer 3 free personal training sessions 5 days a week and a complete selection of cardio equipment, free weights, weight machines and fitness classes.

Additionally, at the end of your workout, whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned individual, you will leave our gym feeling positively refreshed and energised, ready to face the day!

Facilities

All guests over the age of 16 who visit the Asawa Resort are encouraged to make use of our free in-house fitness room to exercise and unwind.



With a plethora of equipment and weights, our gym will cater to your every need, be it a cardio workout, strength training, muscle-building or toning exercises.



Forgot your workout gear? That shouldn't stop you! We have a wide range of high-quality Reebok® footwear and apparel for you available to borrow. Get to burning some of those calories and let's celebrate fitness together!

Training

What if you are in need of a private gym session with a personal trainer? Five days a week, between the hours of 6am to 4pm, we employ the services of leading personal trainers to guide you in your training sessions.



Enjoy personal trainers' advice and guidance during morning hours at no extra cost. If you want to go for that little bit extra, it's also possible to arrange a private session.
