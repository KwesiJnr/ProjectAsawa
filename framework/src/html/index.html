The Perfect Getaway

Hello, and welcome to the Asawa Resort!

Step onto the balcony; bask in the glorious sunshine and inhale the sweet scent of tropical flowers in the courtyard.

With an elevated view on the landscape, you can drink in the stunning greenery and wildlife that stretch for miles around our resort.

Take a moment to wiggle your toes in the grass. This is the moment you have always wanted. Perfect place, perfect food - all away from the humdrum and chaos of urban life.


All of this comes completely hassle-free. You only need to book a reservation and we handle everything. Walk in and walk out, as you please.


We are Asawa, and are happy to have you stay with us!
